# Ramzee - App Feature Overview

> *The social pasture where Rhody Rams graze together*

## What is Ramzee?

Ramzee is a cozy, community-focused social media platform exclusively for students at the **University of Rhode Island**. Think Twitter, but fluffier, friendlier, and full of Ram spirit! Named after our beloved mascot Rhody the Ram, Ramzee is where URI students come to share, connect, and bleat about campus life.

---

## Core Features

### Bleats (Posts)
Forget tweets—Rams **bleat**! Share your thoughts, campus moments, and daily adventures in posts up to 280 characters. Whether you're celebrating acing an exam or complaining about the dining hall, let your voice be heard across the pasture.

- **Text Bleats** - Share quick thoughts and updates
- **Photo Bleats** - Post pictures from around campus
- **Poll Bleats** - Ask the herd for their opinion
- **Thread Bleats** - String together longer stories

### Huffs (Likes)
Show some love with a **Huff**! When you appreciate someone's bleat, give it a huff—like a friendly nod from one Ram to another. Watch the huff counter grow as the herd shows their support.

### Rebaa (Retweets/Reposts)
Spread the word by **Rebaa-ing**! When a bleat deserves more attention, rebaa it to your followers. You can rebaa with or without adding your own commentary.

### Graze (Follow)
**Graze** on content from your favorite Rams! When you graze someone, their bleats appear in your feed. Build your flock by grazing classmates, clubs, and campus personalities.

---

## Social Features

### The Pasture (Home Feed)
Your personalized feed of bleats from everyone you graze. Fresh content delivered straight to your pasture every time you open the app.

### The Meadow (Explore/Discover)
Discover trending topics, popular bleats, and new Rams to graze. The Meadow showcases what's buzzing across campus.

### Herds (Groups)
Join or create **Herds** based on your interests, major, dorm, or clubs:
- *Computer Science Herd*
- *Butterfield Hall Herd*
- *URI Sailing Herd*
- *Late Night Study Crew*

### Barn Chat (Direct Messages)
Take conversations private in the **Barn**. Send direct messages to individuals or create group chats with your closest flock members.

## Campus-Specific Features

### Ram Tags (Hashtags)
Use **#RamTags** to join campus conversations:
- `#RhodyLife`
- `#KingstonWeather`
- `#EmeryDining`
- `#GameDay`

### Verified Rams
Official URI accounts, clubs, and organizations get a special **Golden Fleece** verification badge, so you know you're following the real deal.

### Campus Map Integration
Tag your bleats with locations around campus:
- Memorial Union
- The Quad
- Robert L. Carothers Library
- Ryan Center
- Narragansett Bay Campus

### Event Bleats
Special bleat format for campus events—includes date, time, location, and RSVP tracking. Never miss a club meeting or party again!

## Engagement Features

### Wool Awards (Achievements)
Earn **Wool** for being an active member of the community:
- 🏆 **First Bleat** - Welcome to the pasture!
- 🌟 **Flock Leader** - Reach 100 grazers
- 💬 **Social Ram** - Send 50 barn chats
- 🔥 **Trending Trotter** - Get a bleat on the Meadow
- 🎓 **Senior Ram** - Active for 4+ years

### Streak Counter
Maintain your grazing streak! Log in daily to keep your streak alive and earn bonus wool.

### Rhody Points
Earn **Rhody Points** for engagement—redeem them for campus perks, app customizations, or donate to student organizations.

---

## Safety & Community

### Shepherd Mode (Moderation)
Community-elected **Shepherds** help keep the pasture safe and friendly. Report inappropriate bleats, and Shepherds will review them promptly.

### Flock Block
Easily block or mute Rams who aren't respecting the vibe. Your pasture, your rules.

### Anonymous Bleating (Incognito Ram)
Sometimes you need to share without revealing yourself. Toggle **Incognito Ram** for anonymous posts in designated herds.

### URI Email Verification
Only verified `@uri.edu` email addresses can join Ramzee—keeping the community exclusive to real URI students.
